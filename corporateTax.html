GST/HST Returns & Rebates

GST/HST filing periods may differ for some businesses, in the form of monthly, quarterly, or anually. Depending on the type of your business, different methods of calculations may be requiredto calculate your net tax. As a result, it is important to ensure tax calculations are done correctly to minimize tax liabilities. If you are required to file a GST/HST return, you will still need to do so even if you have no business transactions or no net tax to remit.

Required:

  • Applicable Receipts and Documentation
  • HST account number, HST Access Code, and filing period

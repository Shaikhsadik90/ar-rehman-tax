Front Desk Receptionist - Canada

Job Summary

We are seeking a friendly and organized Receptionist to join our team. The ideal candidate will be the first point of contact for our clients and visitors, providing exceptional customer service while managing various administrative tasks. This role is essential in ensuring smooth operations at the front desk and maintaining a welcoming environment.

Responsibilities

  • Greet and assist clients and visitors in a professional manner.
  • Answer phone calls and direct inquiries appropriately.
  • Perform data entry tasks accurately and efficiently.
  • Manage appointment scheduling and confirmations.
  • Maintain organized filing systems for both physical and electronic documents.
  • Ensure the front desk area is tidy and presentable.

Qualifications

  • Previous experience as a receptionist or admin preferred.
  • Strong customer service and communication skills.
  • Proficient in MS Office Suite or similar programs.
  • Excellent organizational and multitasking abilities.

Job Type: Seasonal (4 months)

Hours: 35 – 44 per week (may increase as needed)

Location: In person
